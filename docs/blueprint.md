# **App Name**: Benaiah Academy

## Core Features:

- Homepage: Display key information, a welcome message, and quick links to other sections of the website.
- About Page: Provide an overview of Benaiah Academy, its mission, values, and history.
- Programs Page: Showcase the different programs offered at Benaiah Academy, including curriculum details and age groups served.
- Our Journey Page: Present a timeline of Benaiah Academy's milestones, achievements, and events.
- Contact Page: Allow parents and visitors to reach out to the school through a contact form or displayed contact information (email, phone number, address).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Sunny Yellow (#FFEB3B) for happiness and friendliness.
- Background color: Very light yellow (#FAF8DD), a desaturated tint of the primary color.
- Accent color: Sky blue (#87CEEB) for a bright, complementary contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' for readability and a friendly modern look.
- Use simple, colorful icons related to children and education, ensuring they are easily recognizable.
- Design a clean, intuitive layout with ample white space to avoid visual clutter. Organize information into clear sections.
- Incorporate subtle, child-friendly animations for interactive elements like button hovers and transitions between pages.
